Introduction

The conviction behind the mission.

We are living in a world that increasingly exploits our differences. Division is loud. Connection takes work. People Centric Leadership exists because understanding what people truly value, and how they perceive each other's values, is one of the most powerful tools we have for bringing people together.

Every decision — from colour to copy, from typography to tone — is rooted in five personality traits: grounded, human, precise, quietly courageous, and purposefully warm.

"These guidelines are the foundation of the brand. Use them with consistency and confidence to build the future of connection."

Photography

PCL photography should feel like a documentary filmmaker captured a genuine moment of connection. Avoid flat lighting; shadows add depth and honesty.

"See the whole person. Candid, raw, and unposed. Subjects are real people in real settings."

"Subtle references reward those who know the story."

The Three Stars

A recurring motif denoting excellence and achievement. They appear in the brand mark and serve as a compositional principle for layouts.

The Stag

Denoting courage and resilience. This is expressed through the use of Golden Orange, reserved for moments of real weight and decision.
