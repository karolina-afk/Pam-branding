Introduction

About This Document

People Centric Leadership (PCL) exists because too many organisations treat values as a tick-box exercise, too many leaders operate without credible evidence, and too many voices go unheard.

These guidelines capture our strategic identity. Every decision—from colour to copy, from typography to tone—is rooted in five personality traits that define this brand: grounded, soulful, precise, quietly courageous, and purposefully warm.

"Use these guidelines as a compass, not a cage."

Positioning

We listen to what people truly value, share what they tell us without judgement, and equip leaders to connect with their people through credible evidence — openly, for good.

PCL Brand Identity

Brand Personality

Grounded, soulful, precise, quietly courageous, and purposefully warm. The brand doesn't shout; it speaks with purpose.

Brand as a Product

Evidence-first research reports and insights that bridge the gap between academic research and commercial consultancy.

Brand as an Organisation

Independent and self-funded, partnering only with organisations whose values align with its own.
