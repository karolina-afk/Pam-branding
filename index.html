Introduction

About This Document

We are living in a world that increasingly exploits our differences. Division is loud. Connection takes work. People Centric Leadership exists because of a conviction — built over decades of leadership experience — that understanding what people truly value, and how they perceive each other's values, is one of the most powerful tools we have for bringing people together.

These guidelines capture the identity built to carry that mission into the world. Every decision — from colour to copy, from typography to tone — is rooted in five personality traits that define this brand: grounded, human, precise, quietly courageous, and purposefully warm.

"These guidelines are a living document. As PCL grows and its audience widens, they will develop alongside it. For now, they are the foundation of the brand and should be used with consistency and confidence."

PCL Brand Identity

Brand Personality

PCL carries the poise of a seasoned leader and the soul of a storyteller. Grounded, human, precise, quietly courageous, and purposefully warm — these five traits shape every interaction. The brand doesn't shout, oversell, or perform. It shows up fully, speaks with purpose, and always leaves room for others. Like wind, it adapts and flows through resistance with calm, grounded articulation rather than force.

Brand as a Product

PCL delivers freely accessible, evidence-first research reports and leadership insights that bridge the gap between abstract academic research and commercial consultancy. Its unique lens explores not just what people value, but how similar or different they believe others' values to be. Extended offerings include workshops, advisory sessions, sector-specific materials, and youth outreach tools. Every product is designed to make rigorous evidence feel accessible and human, never intimidating.

Brand as an Organisation

PCL is independent and self-funded, partnering only with organisations whose values align with its own. It operates across sectors and generations, proactively seeking new audiences rather than waiting for them to arrive. Founded on the conviction that research insights should be freely available, it serves as a megaphone for people's voices — never the judge of their values. PCL exists to unlock the conditions for better leadership, stronger teams, and a more harmonious society.

Logotype

People Centric Leadership

(PCL Master Logo consists of the brand mark and the wordmark "People Centric Leadership" set in Cormorant Garamond. They work together as a single unit.)

Logo Concepts

  • The Compass Star: Three overlapping angular shapes forming a compass rose with an emergent star at centre.
  • The Wind Lines: Flowing, parallel curved lines sweeping left to right, evoking adaptable, kinetic movement.
  • The Open Circle: A circle deliberately not closed, conveying welcome and openness: there is room for you here.

Exclusion Zones & Sizing

To ensure legibility and standout, retain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'P' in the wordmark on all sides. Minimum digital width is 120px; minimum print width is 30mm.

Why Cormorant Garamond?

Tall, elegant proportions and fine detail. Chosen to express the Sage archetype through letterforms—scholarly precision with a distinctly human touch. Used exclusively for headlines, hero statements, and primary navigation.

Why Lora?

A contemporary serif with roots in calligraphy. Grounds the elegant Cormorant with a slightly sturdier, more modern body. Its brushed curves add subtle warmth to extended reading, balancing scholarship with soul. Used for all body copy and subtext.

Photography

See the Whole Person

Candid and raw, with warmth and intention. PCL photography should feel like a documentary filmmaker captured a genuine moment of connection. Subjects are real people in real settings—never actors in staged boardrooms.

Lighting & Composition

Natural and directional—golden-hour warmth, soft window light, or gentle overcast. Avoid flat, even lighting. Shadows add depth and honesty. Balanced and symmetrical where possible, but never rigid. Leave breathing space.

Heritage Elements

Three Stars

Denoting excellence, achievement, and noble aspirations. Appears as a recurring motif in the brand mark and as a compositional principle.

The Stag

Denoting courage, strength, and resilience. Expressed quietly through our Golden Orange accents, reserved for moments of real weight.
