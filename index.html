Introduction

About This Document

We are living in a world that increasingly exploits our differences. Division is loud. Connection takes work. People Centric Leadership exists because of a conviction — built over decades of leadership experience — that understanding what people truly value, and how they perceive each other's values, is one of the most powerful tools we have for bringing people together.

These guidelines capture the identity built to carry that mission into the world. Every decision — from colour to copy, from typography to tone — is rooted in five personality traits that define this brand: grounded, human, precise, quietly courageous, and purposefully warm.

"These guidelines are a living document. As PCL grows and its audience widens, they will develop alongside it. For now, they are the foundation of the brand."

PCL Brand Identity

Brand Personality

Grounded, human, precise, quietly courageous, and purposefully warm. The brand doesn't shout; it shows up fully, speaks with purpose, and always leaves room for others. Like wind, it adapts and flows through resistance.

Brand as a Product

Freely accessible research reports and leadership insights. PCL bridges the gap between academic research and commercial consultancy, making rigorous evidence feel accessible and human.

Brand as an Organisation

Independent and self-funded. PCL exists to serve as a megaphone for people's voices — never the judge of their values. We unlock the conditions for a more harmonious society.
